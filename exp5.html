Title : Centroid 1-D

Aim: To verify the Parallelogram Law of Forces

Apparatus: Metal Wire, string, scale, blank paper, protractors, etc.

Theory: Centoid of 1-D object is known as the point at which entire mass of the object is concentrated.



x = ∑Lixi ∑L i

y = ∑Liyi ∑L i

Where, (x,y) is the coordinates of the centroid of the 1-D object

Procedure:

  • Take a metal strip wire and turn it into given shape.
  • Make a trace the wire shape on blank paper
  • Plot the surface reference axes for the traced figure.
  • Calculate centroid analytically for given traced object.
  • Repeate the above procedure by hanging the object at any other point and then trace the gravity line on the paper
  • Intersections on the both traced line given as the centroid on object experimentally.
  • Find coordinates of the experimental centroid using the scale from the reference axis.
  • Calculate errors in analytical and experimental centroid coordinates.
